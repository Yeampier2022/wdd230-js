Target Audience

Audience:

The target audience is a 25-50 year old traveler with middle to high income, college education, winter sports experience and access to a computer or laptop. Their purpose is to research ski and snowboard destinations to plan an exciting winter vacation.

Peoples

Persona 1: Adventure Enthusiast.

  • Age: 25-40.
  • Income: $50,000-$100,000.
  • Education: College degree or higher.
  • Experience with the topic: With adventure.
  • Family status: Married with children.

Persona 2: Family Vacation Planner.

  • Age: 35-50.
  • Income: $75,000-$150,000.
  • Education: College degree or higher.
  • Experience with the topic: With adventure.
  • Family status: Married with children.

Scenarios

1. Researching Adventure Destinations: A visitor wants to plan a winter vacation that includes skiing and snowboarding.

2.Planning a Group Adventure: A group of friends want to plan a hiking trip for the summer.

3. Seeking Information on Adventure Activities: A visitor is interested in trying rock climbing for the first time and wants to learn.

4. Booking Adventure Tours: A visitor wants to book a whitewater rafting tour for their upcoming vacation.

5. Finding Deals and Discounts: A visitor is looking to save money on their next adventure vacation.
